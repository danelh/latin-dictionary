+Corona classica+ or +rostrata+ was the crown given to a commanding officer who had gained a great victory at sea.
* Insigne coronæ classicæ, quo nemo unquam Romanorum donatus erat, hoc bello Agrippa singulari virtute meruit. #Suet.#
+Corona navalis+, a crown formed like beaks of ships, given to him who first boarded an enemy's ship.
* Tempora navali fulgent rostrata corona. #Virg.#
