+Demum+, %at length%, %after a long time%.
* Nunc demum litteris tuis rescribo. #Cic.#
_Demum_ is also taken in a sense analogous to _solum_, _tantum_, %only%.
* Ea demum magna voluptas est, æqualem ac parem verbis vitam agere. #Cic.#
+Denique+, %in fine%, is placed at the end of a long enumeration.
* Non avaritia, non libido, non amœnitas, non nobilitas urbis, non denique labor, &c. #Cic.#
+Tandem+, %when a thing has been a great while longed-after%.
* Tandem progreditur magna stipante caterva. #Virg.#
* Ibis tandem aliquando, quo te, &c. #Cic.#
