+Potus+, %drink%, %drinking%.
* Cum ea pars animi sit immoderato obstupefacta potu atque pastu. #Cic.#
+Potio+, %the act of drinking%.
* In media potione exclamavit mulier, se mori. #Cic.#
It is said of all kinds of drink, even of a medical draught.
* Multo cibo et potione completi. #Cic.#
* Medicus prima potione sustulit mulierem, #Id.#, The physician killed that woman by the first draught he gave her.
+Potatio+, (_frequentative_ of potio) %the act of drinking hard, of drunkenness%.
* Hesterna potatione oscitans. #Cic.#
