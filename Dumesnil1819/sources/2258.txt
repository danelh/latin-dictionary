+Sinus+, %the bosom%, %all within the compass of the breast and arms%, %all above the girdle%.
* Fovere dextram sinu. #Ovid.#
Figuratively:
* In sinu gaudere. #Cic.#
* Calumniatores de sinu suo apposuit. #Id.#
* Cæsar mihi in sinu est. #Id.#
It is said of what has many folds.
* Nodoque sinus collecta fluentes. #Virg.#
* Pandentemque sinus et tota veste vocantem cæruleum in gremium. #Id.#
It is also said of a gulf or bay.
* Ater sinus Adriae. #Hor.#
+Gremium+, (quasi geremium, _from_ gerere) %the lap%, %the space between the girdle to the knees of a person sitting%.
* Qui se in suo gremio positurum puerum dicebat. #Ter.#
* Puer lactens, in gremio matris sedens. #Cic.#
Figuratively:
* E sinu gremioque patriæ abstrahi. #Cic.#
* Thessalonicenses positi in gremio imperii nostri. #Id.#
* Puer lactens in sinu matris incumbit; sedet in gremio. #G. D.#
