+Elidere+, (lædere e) properly %to force out by striking%, %to bruise%.
* Angit inhærens elisos oculos. #Virg.#
* Caput pecudis saxo elidit. #Liv.#
* Matri denarrat ut ingens bellua cognatos eliserit, #Hor.#, The young frog relates to her mother how a huge beast had just crushed her sisters to pieces.
Figuratively:
* Poëtæ nervos omnes virtutis elidunt. #Cic.#
* Elidi ægritudine. #Id.#
+Suffocare+, (sub fauces) %to suffocate%, %to throttle%.
* Gallum gallinaceum suffocare. #Cic.#
Figuratively:
* Fame suffocare urbem. #Cic.#
+Strangulare+, (stringere gulam) %to strangle%, %to take away life by stifling%.
* Is patrem strangulavit. #Cic.#
Figuratively:
* Strangulat segetes nimia terræ lætitia, #Cic.#, When a field is too fertile, it stifles the corn.
