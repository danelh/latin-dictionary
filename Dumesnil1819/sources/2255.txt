+Singuli+, %one by one%, %one after another%.
+Universi+, %all together and in general%, %all without exception%.
* Dum singuli pugnant, universi vincuntur, #Tac.#, Fighting only one after another, they are all conquered in the end.
* Quae si singula vos forte non movent, universa certe tamen inter se conjuncta atque connexa movere debebunt. #Cic.#
* Deus separatim ab universis singulos diligit. #Id.#
