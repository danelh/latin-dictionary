*Trans*, 783, 829, %on the other side%, along above that which is this side to that which is on 
the other side;
*Ultra*, %the other side%, beyond that which is on the other side:
* =Trans= Tiberim hortos parare. #Cic.#
* Ariovistus præter castra Cæsaris suas copias transduxit, et millibus passuum duobus =ultra= eum castra fecit. #Cæs.#, first he had the camp of Cæsar on this side; now he encamped far beyond it, on the other side.
