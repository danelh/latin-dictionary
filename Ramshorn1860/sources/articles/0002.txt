To hide by change of place is *Abdere*, %to do away%, %to hide%, e. g. +se in sylvas+;
*Condere*, %to put together%, %to keep and preserve%:
* Testudo =abdidit= cornea corpus domo, nec lædi potest =condita=. #Phædr.#
*Abscondere*, %to keep%, preserve in a hidden place, *Recondere*, in a remote, distant place: +Res abscondita+; something kept hidden, of which we do not allow others to know; +literæ reconditæ+, those which we keep for ourselves and allow rarely to be seen.
*Abstrudere*, %to push%, drive away and into a depth;
*Retrudere*, into a remote deep corner:
* Me in silvam =abstrusi= densam. #Cic.#

To hide by covering: *Occŭlere*, %to envelope%, %veil%; *Occultare*, XIX, 10, b, %to hide carefully%:
* Alcibiades penitus in Thraciam se =abdidit=, sperans ibi suam fortunam =occuli= posse. #Nep.#
* Natura partes corporis turpes contexit atque =abdidit=; quæ autem =occultavit=, eadem omnes removent ab oculis. #Cic.#
