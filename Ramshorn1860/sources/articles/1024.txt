*Virgo*, %the maiden in her full vigor%;
*Puella*, %a girl%, refers to the early youthful age of the female:
* =Virgo= adulta, incorrupta. #Cic.#
* Verba =puellarum=, foliis leviora caducis. #Ovid.#
both words are used likewise of young married women:
* Ah, =virgo= infelix, tu nunc in montibus erras. #Virg.#, of Pasiphae, wife of Minos.
* Poppæa, =puella= vicesimo ætatis anno. #Tac.#
*Vǐrāgo*, %a masculine woman%, %a heroine%:
* Belli metuenda =virago=. #Ovid.#, i. e. Minerva.
