*Ultro* (see 984), %in addition%, %above expectation%, %even%, used of free actions which go beyond 
the natural limit of their relations:
* Mithridatem Asia non modo recipiebat suis urbibus, verum etiam =ultro= vocabat. #Cic.#
*Sponte*, %spontaneously%, %from free impulse%, with the consent and assent of some one, by itself, of itself, without cause:
* Sua =sponte= et voluntate quidpiam facere. #Cic.#
* Sua =sponte=, nulla adhibita vi, consumptus ignis exstinguitur. #Id.#
